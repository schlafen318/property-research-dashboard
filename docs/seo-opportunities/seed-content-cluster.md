# Seed SEO Content Cluster

These are human-review landing page candidates for the first SEO growth phase. They are based on known high-intent buyer themes, not Search Console rows. Do not auto-merge these into published pages without editorial review.

## Best Countries to Buy Property as a Foreigner

- Target keyword: `best countries to buy property as a foreigner`
- Proposed URL: `/best-countries-to-buy-property-as-a-foreigner/`
- Intent: foreign buyers comparing legal access, title clarity, transaction practicality, and lifestyle quality before choosing countries for deeper diligence.
- Proposed internal links: `/buy-property-abroad/`, `/best-countries-for-expats-to-buy-property/`, `/methodology/`, `/research-standards/`, relevant destination pages with strong ownership scores.
- FAQ candidates: Which countries let foreigners buy property? What legal risks should foreign buyers check first? Is freehold ownership always better than leasehold?
- Acceptance criteria: page uses existing destination data, has unique title/meta/H1/canonical, includes FAQ schema, links to trust pages, and does not make legal or tax claims beyond the existing research caveat.
