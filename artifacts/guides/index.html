How to Use These Guides

Start with the page that matches the job of the property, then compare the linked destination dossiers before talking to local agents. The goal is to avoid falling in love with a listing before the market, ownership path, rental rules, healthcare practicality, and resale depth have been tested.

Every guide connects back to the same 10-dimension model: lifestyle magnetism, global access, ownership clarity, regulatory safety, rental profit, capital upside, retirement fit, exit liquidity, foreigner fit, and value entry. That keeps broad searches comparable instead of turning each market into a separate story.
